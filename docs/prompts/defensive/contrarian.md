# The Contrarian Challenger

**Category:** defensive
**Source:** Critical thinking

## When to Use

Breaking out of echo chambers or stress-testing a thesis.

## The Prompt

```
Here is my current thesis/strategy: [Insert Strategy].

Act as a highly intelligent, rational contrarian. Your goal is to completely dismantle my thesis. 

Provide the 3 strongest arguments against my strategy, backed by logical reasoning or historical precedent. 

Tell me why I am wrong.
```

## How It Works

LLMs generally want to agree with the user. Explicitly commanding it to be a contrarian overrides this sycophantic tendency and produces genuinely critical analysis.
